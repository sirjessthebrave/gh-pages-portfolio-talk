Your Personal Brand

NOTES

What kind of developer you want to be

When starting to craft your portfolio and pick out the reasons you stand out - it is important to think about that in terms of a personal brand. What are you good at? For me it is building community and speaking plainly - I build my brand as an organizer and someone who speaks honestly about the emotionality in tech work. Are you an introvert? Find open source code to contribute to. Extroverted? Be an meetup organizer. Once you develop your personal brand - make it consistant acrross all your platforms.
